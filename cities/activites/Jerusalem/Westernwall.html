About

The Western Wall, located in Jerusalem's Old City, is a sacred site in Judaism and a symbol of Jewish heritage. Visitors often come to pray and leave written prayers in its crevices. It holds deep historical and spiritual significance. The wall is part of the remains of the ancient Jewish Temple.

Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Address: Old City, Jerusalem, Israel

Prices: Free entry
